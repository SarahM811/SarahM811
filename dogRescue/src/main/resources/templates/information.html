Things you need to know before adopting a pup

Getting a pup is exciting! However, before you jump in your car and adopt a puppy, there are lot of things you would like to consider.  


First, finance. Dog is a lifetime companion. They will be loyal and always by your side until their last day. They will need bowls, they need to eat, and they might need a bed as well! They also need preventative medications which can be costly.  They might get sick, just like humans. Dogs also need to be regularly checked for their health and vaccinations. Please take that into consideration.

Second, training. Every dog has a different personality and different traits. Some dogs can be toy possessive and some can be territorial. However, that does not make them bad dogs. Any dogs can be trained. It will require patience and some might require a professional training. You may need to teach your dogs certain behaviors and attitudes.
