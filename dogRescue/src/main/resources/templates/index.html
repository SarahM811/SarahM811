Events


Events are a great opportunity for you to meet available dogs in person. This will give you a great sense of temperament of each dog as well as will give you an opportunity to connect with the dogs.

Adopting a dog comes with a great responsibility. Please know that your pup will be there for a long time.  You will be able to meet your lifelong companion!

Do you have children? Do not be afriad! Every dog can be trained- with that said, dogs may need to be trained to learn basic etiquettes to be gentle with little children. HOwever, some dogs just know and they will already be gentle with children.
